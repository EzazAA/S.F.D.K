Screen Face distance Keeper

INTRODUCTION

In this evolving world of technology, it has become mandatory to use gadgets (e.g. Phone, Laptop, PC etc.). They have many benefits such as , we get to know about the whole world sitting in any corner of the world, gaining information has became much easier. But these gadgets also have a dark side. Almost most of the gadgets we use have a screen.

These screens either OLED or AMOLED while using them these screens emit Blue light. This high energy blue light passes through the cornea and lens reaching the retina, causing diseases such as Dry eye, Eye strain, Headache etc

Afterwards I did some research on cause and preventions of this, and I came up with this Idea.

Scientific principle involved

Here, the Ultrasonic sensor Emits Ultrasonic sound waves ( About 40,000hz) and receives the reflected waves to measure the distance using the time taken. Then it sends this data to The microcontroller board, The board processes the data and sends it to The led s and the HC-05. The Bluetooth Module Sends the data to the device. Where the app processes it send the notification needed.

Hardware

  1. Wooden case
  2. Arduino Nano
  3. Ultrasonic sensor( HCSR-04)
  4. HC-05( Bluetooth Module)
  5. LED s
  6. 9V Power supply(conditions apply)
  7. Wires
  8. 1k resistors
  9. Switch(conditions apply)
